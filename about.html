Our Story

Debbie Pearl, based in Huntington Beach, California, is a dedicated dog trainer and rehabilitator renowned for her commitment to rescuing and rehabilitating dogs with disabilities. Her journey began with a career in training dogs for film and television through her company, Paws for Effect. This path led her to establish Dream Fetchers, a non-profit organization focused on animal-assisted therapy, bringing comfort and joy to individuals in hospitals, schools, and other community settings. Pearl’s mission took a profound turn when she rescued Eddie, a terrier mix found dragging his paralyzed back legs after suffering severe spinal trauma. Despite initial challenges, Eddie’s resilience inspired Pearl to adopt and rehabilitate more dogs with disabilities, leading to the formation of “The Unstoppable Dogs.” This group has grown to thirteen dogs, all victims of abuse, each with unique stories of survival and recovery. Through Dream Fetchers and now The Unstoppable Ranch, Pearl and The Unstoppable Dogs provide animal-assisted therapy, visiting nursing homes, hospitals, rehabilitation centers, charity events, and schools to inspire and bring joy to others. Their story exemplifies resilience, compassion, and the profound bond between humans and animals. Now the goal is, with your help, to build a real brick and mortar Unstoppable Ranch; a sanctuary where humans and disabled dogs inspire each other and the unstoppable force of love knows no bounds.
